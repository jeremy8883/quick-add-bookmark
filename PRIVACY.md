# Privacy Policy — Quick Add Bookmark

**Last updated:** April 24, 2026

## Summary

Quick Add Bookmark does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside of your browser.

## Data Usage

This extension accesses the following browser data solely to provide its core functionality:

- **Bookmarks**: Read and modified to let you add, edit, and organize bookmarks.
- **Active Tab**: The title, URL, and favicon of the current tab are read to pre-fill the bookmark form.
- **Local Storage**: Your last-used folder selection is saved locally in the browser so it can be restored next time.

## Data Storage

All data remains in your browser using Chrome's built-in `chrome.bookmarks` and `chrome.storage.local` APIs. No data is sent to any external server, third-party service, or analytics platform.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| `bookmarks` | Create, edit, move, and delete bookmarks |
| `activeTab` | Read the current tab's title, URL, and favicon |
| `storage` | Remember your last-used folder selection |

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking, or advertising.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be posted here in this repository.
